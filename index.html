PLANNING PHASE

First, and most important part of a smoothly project ist communication! I will make sure, that you always know what im doing and how i am doing it. On the other hand, with every color palette, every layout and every content i create, you will be involved in the progress. That keeps you updated and avoids big changes at the end.


Furthermore, besides communication, i have another, almost equally important key attribute for a succesful project: its preparation! Before i even open my IDE, i answer myself a lot of questions, some of those are:


  • what do I want to show my audience?
  • what exactly is is my target audience?
  • how am i going to present my content (wireframes)
  • what content fits best to achieve my goals?
  • how can i underline my content with visual elements?
